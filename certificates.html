Completion Certificates

Our completion certificates reflect the trust our clients place in us and the consistent quality we deliver. Each certificate is a mark of successful collaboration, timely execution, and professional excellence across a wide range of projects in Rwanda and beyond.

2019

Certificate of Completion – National Bank of Rwanda (Musanze Branch)

L&F Consultants was awarded a tender for design review and construction supervision of the Musanze Branch. The National Bank of Rwanda was fully satisfied with the services rendered.

2015

Certificate of Good Completion – EPR Kigali Presbytery Temple

L&F Consultants successfully completed architectural and engineering design for the EPR Temple in Gisozi. Services were delivered professionally and with integrity.

2006

Certificate of Satisfactory Completion – Kigali Free Trade Zone

L&F Consultants performed engineering design services for the Kigali Free Trade Zone, including utility plans for water, electricity, wastewater, and telecom networks. Work was completed to the client’s satisfaction.
